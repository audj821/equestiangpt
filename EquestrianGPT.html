The EquestrianGPT

Before jumping into what this resource is, I would love to share a brief personal anecdote. One time, several years ago, I was at an AQHA show in Pennsylvania. My at the time trainer had brought me and several other students with her to get horses qualified for the world show that year. Well, it's important to note that at the time I was showing a grey paint mare. Now, if you kow about gray horses in particular, you will be able to note that as they age, the horse does infact get lighter. At this point the horse I was showing was practically completetly white with just a very light hint of grey. Which fine, in the events we showed in nobody batted an eye.

That was until I stepped into the halter pen to try my hand at the circuit show's highpoint award. I learned very quickly, though disqualification, that 1. "excessive white" is not appreciated by the judges. (Let's just say there's a reason most halter horses are brown) and 2. Eventhough her hooves were striped, some were solid black whereas others were black and white, they required her hooves to be clear coated. As it stood when I was showing, the judges deemed it as artifically augmenting the horse's natural appearance.

I add this story because I think it highlights perfectly the exact reason I started this project. I was thankful that it hapened at a state level show, bbut if it were Congress? That small mistake could have potentially costed my family hundreds of dollars in entry fees. This niche knowledge is far from common, but it should be far more easily acessible than burried far in the association handbook.

What is EquestrianGPT, and How Is It Used?

EquestrianGPT is an OpenAI Large Language Model which exclusively uses handbooks from numerous organizations to give you the answers to the questions you may have.

Associations Equestrian GPT is trained on:

  • AQHA (American Quarter Horse Association)
  • APHA (American Paint Horse Association)
  • NSBA (National Snaffle Bit Association)
  • PtHA (Pinto Horse Association)
  • USEF (United States Equestrian Federation)
